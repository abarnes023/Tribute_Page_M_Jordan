Air Jordan | The greatest of all time

Michael Jordan was one of the most competitive players in the NBA of all time. His drive to win led him to greatness.

Jordan's NBA Highlights

  • 1984-Jordan is drafted by the Chicago Bulls with the third overall pick
  • 1985-the former UNC Tarheels star wins NBA Rookie of the Year
  • 1987-88-Averaging 35.0 ppg, leading the NBA, Jordan wins his first MVP award
  • 1991-The Bulls defeat the LA Lakers in the NBA finals under MJ's leadership
  • 1992-Jordan wins his second NBA finals and second Olympic Gold Medal with the "Dream Team"
  • 1993-Following the murder of his father, Michael Jordan announces his retirement from the NBA after winning the 1992-93 NBA finals
  • 1996-98-Jordan wins his 4th, 5th, and 6th NBA Finals and Finals MVP Awards
  • 2001-03-Comes out of retirement to play with the Washington Wizards
  • 2006-Managing member of Charlotte Bobcats
